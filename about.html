About Me

Hi, I’m Michael Obe known as “Techkidd” a 5 years experienced Product Designer and UX Researcher, I love creating digital designs and meeting my customers needs by breathing life into shapes.

My Journey

Passionate about about visual design and emerging technology. I specialize in UX/UI Design, UX research, Interaction designs and my passion is all about solving complex problems through data driven process by understanding users empathy and creating seamless human-centred design to help meet your business goals.

My objective is to expand my boundaries and gather more experience and knowledge , seeking a position where I can contribute to organizations by using advanced skills in Product Design and creativity to produce a unique and quality product for the user. I am currently based in Lagos, Nigeria, working with people and businesses all over the world.

How I work

As a Product Designer, I focus on ways to improve customer’s experience and the user’s overall satisfaction with a product.

I design seamless interfaces by; Understanding the user and brand, conducting user research (identifying user needs, goals, behaviours and pain points) which include taking surveys, one-to-one interviews and A/B Testing.

I take my time to build user personas based on the research to help me identify the most important elements of the product or service.

After conducting research, I create site maps, wireframes, or prototypes to give myself and the team a better idea of what the final product will look like.

I then validate the design by tracking how real users interact with the product or service (usability testing) and Identify any problems with the design and develop solutions.
